v0.4 - June 20 2023
What? ANOTHER Powerset update??? Yup! I was playing it the other day and noticed the weird bug where you couldn't always take opposing pieces in the top row, so I found what was causing it and fixed it! (Also, wow is my old code messy.) Moved the powerbar numbers to the middle so they don't look so lazy, added a "Highscore" feature, made a color benchmark system based on how high your score is (Copper (900), Gold (1500), and Platnium (2000), Silver wasn't very visible so I didn't include it). Might add some more small stuff later!
v0.3.1 - November 21 2021
Just some mini-updates I made since May, one of them being the point system! Now, instead of each opposing pawn being worth 100 points or so - I forgot how many lol - each pawn is now worth more the farther away from your side you take it. For example, if you take a pawn right after it reaches your side, it will only be worth 10 points, but if you take it right when it comes out on the board, it's worth 70 points. You can see the numbers for each row on the Power Bar. Now, after the game ends, the powerbar will adjust to the mean (average) number of points you recieved for each piece taken, the higher the powerbar is, the more pieces you took from the opposing side of the board. One more small change is a shadow around the game window, which is subtly darker in light mode, and glows in dark mode.
v0.3 - May 23 2021
What? A Powerset update? Indeed. I finally took the courage to dive into every programmers fear - their old code. And luckily, I was able to find the reason to why the right most helper sometimes didn't disapear when it was taken (the list which counted for which pieces were taken was one number short) Any changes? An easy one, actually - the addition of the Mappist difficulty! It's super easy and gives a frustrated player something to do while they aggravate over why they can't get over the score 3000. This game feels so long ago, but it hasn't even been 2 years since I last touched it, so maybe time isn't going by as quick as I think it is :)
